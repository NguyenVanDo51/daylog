15 screens, one consistent vocabulary:
• 2.5px ink border + 3-4px offset shadow (no blur) on every interactive surface.
• 🐱 mascot is on sign-in, home header, settings avatar, empty states, onboarding.
• Tilted cards (-1° to +1°) on lists and grids — sparingly, not chaotic.
• Sticker chips for state: counts, dates, "Chủ" badge, video markers, selected filters.
• Comic-cursive headlines, Baloo 2 for body. All Vietnamese-first.
